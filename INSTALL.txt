Dependencies:

 Thats the configuration PloneFormMailer is tested with. It might work with
 older versions too.

 * Plone 2.0.4 with it's dependencies (esp. Formulator and CMFFormController).
 
 * Archetypes 1.3 >= RC1 

 * gpg binary (optional) 

 * LinguaPlone (optional)

Installation:

 Untar in your 'Products' directory in filesystem. Restart Zope. Go to 
 'Add/Remove Products' in 'Plone Setup' or 'portal_quickinstaller' in 
 ZMI and just click install.
